About This Program

The Malaysia Seafarer Program is a certified maritime training program open to all individuals interested in a seafaring career. The program covers technical training, professional certifications, and a 6-month practical placement onboard a vessel or ferry for the Complete Package.

🎂

Age 18 – 35 Years

Age Requirement
Applicant must be at least 18 years old on the date of registration.
Maximum age is 35 years old at the time of application.
Age is verified based on the National ID (MyKad) or Passport date of birth.
Applicants approaching 35 are encouraged to register promptly as seats are limited.
💡 Required document: Copy of valid MyKad or Passport.
👁️

No Colour Blindness

Vision Test
Applicant must pass the Ishihara Colour Vision Test — standard maritime vision screening.
Mandatory requirement under international STCW (Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping).
Colour blindness can pose serious safety risks when identifying navigation lights and maritime signals.
Test is conducted by a certified doctor during the Phase 1 Medical Check Up.
💡 Vision test is automatically conducted during the Phase 1 medical examination.
📖

Literate & Numerate

Basic Skills
Able to read and understand text in Bahasa Malaysia and/or basic English.
Able to write clearly for ship logbooks, reports and daily records.
Basic numeracy skills required for navigation, measurements and operational records.
Basic English is an advantage as international maritime documentation uses English.
💡 No minimum academic qualification required — basic literacy is sufficient to enroll.
💪

Physically Fit

Health & Fitness
Free from chronic illnesses that may affect performance onboard a vessel.
No serious mental health conditions that could affect safety at sea.
Able to perform physical tasks — climbing, lifting equipment and working in varied weather.
Free from drug and alcohol dependency. Drug screening will be conducted.
Medical Fitness Certificate issued by a MOH-certified physician is required.
💡 Full medical examination is conducted during Phase 1 before training commences.

🏥

Marine Medical Check Up

Maritime Medical Examination

✓Comprehensive physical examination by a certified maritime physician.
✓Vision test including colour vision (Ishihara Test).
✓Hearing test and blood pressure assessment.
✓Basic laboratory tests (blood and urine analysis).
✓Medical Fitness Certificate recognised by Jabatan Laut Malaysia (JLM).
✓Certificate is valid for 2 years from date of examination.
🪪

Seaman Card

Seafarer Identity Document

✓Official seafarer identity card issued by Jabatan Laut Malaysia.
✓Required to board a vessel and undertake practical placement.
✓Required documents: MyKad, passport-sized photo, medical certificate.
✓Application process takes approximately 1–2 working weeks.
✓Must be valid and active before enrolling in Phase 2 courses.
✓CTR can assist with the Seaman Card application process.
⚠️

Important — Please Read Before Registering

Both Phase 1 documents (Marine Medical Check Up and Seaman Card) must be obtained and verified before you can enroll in Phase 2 courses. Registration for Phase 2 cannot be processed without these documents. Contact CTR for guidance.
